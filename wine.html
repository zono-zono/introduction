Napa Valley Red Blend

Rindo

紫鈴

KENZO ESTATE · 2020

A Bordeaux-style blend from Kenzo Estate in Napa Valley. The mouthfeel is remarkably smooth, and the moment it touches your lips, a gentle sweetness blooms and spreads. The kind of wine that feels effortless to drink yet stays with you long after the glass is empty.

ナパヴァレーのケンゾーエステートが手がけるボルドースタイルのブレンドワイン。口当たりが滑らかで、飲んだ瞬間に甘さがふわっと広がる。するすると飲めてしまうのに、余韻は長く続く一本。
